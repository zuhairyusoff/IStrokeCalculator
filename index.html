    Suggestions indicate follow-up intensity and referral threshold, not an independent indication to start new pharmacotherapy. Treatment decisions should still be justified against standard eligibility criteria in the cited guidelines for the individual patient.
    Research tool derived from the Pulau Pinang National Diabetes Registry (2019–2024), DrPH thesis, Universiti Teknologi MARA. Intended to support, not replace, clinical judgement. Risk tiers: Low ≤14 points, Medium 15–19 points, High ≥20 points. The AI calibrated probability uses a logistic recalibration layer fitted on the most recent validated registry audit and should be refreshed periodically as new audit data become available; the points allocation itself does not change between recalibrations.
